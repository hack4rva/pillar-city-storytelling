## 1. Artsy

- **Founded:** 2009 by Carter Cleveland [1]. 
- **Mission:** "Expand the art market to support more artists" – the platform blends a marketplace with a data-driven editorial engine and the proprietary **Art Genome** taxonomy that matches collectors with works [1]. 
- **Key Numbers:** 1.6M+ artworks, 3,100+ partner galleries, 3.4M+ registered users [1]. 
- **Why it matters:** Artsy is the largest online art-marketplace by inventory and user base, making it a primary channel for galleries seeking global exposure [1]. 

---

## 2. Artnet

- **Founded:** 1990 by Hans Neuendorf [2]. 
- **Core Services:** Comprehensive **Price Database** (10M+ auction results), news, and a secondary-marketplace [2]. 
- **Scale:** Serves over 2.1M monthly users across 1,700+ auction houses [2]. 
- **Strategic Note:** Early mover in digitising auction data; still the industry benchmark for price transparency [2]. 

---

## 3. Saatchi Art

- **Founded:** 2010-05 (as *Saatchi Online*, later acquired by Demand Media) [3]. 
- **Offering:** Online gallery + **The Other Art Fair** integration + hospitality-art advisory for hotels & restaurants [3]. 
- **Impact:** Over 100,000 artists have sold works through the platform; 14 years of continuous growth [4]. 

---

## 4. Artprice

- **Founded:** 1987 by Thierry Ehrmann [5]. 
- **Products:** Auction-price database (30M+ results), market reports, and a fixed-price marketplace launched in 2004 [5]. 
- **Relevance:** The most extensive European-centric auction database; frequently cited in art-market research [5]. 

---

## 5. 1stDibs

- **Founded:** 2001 by Michael Bruno [6]. 
- **Focus:** Luxury-goods marketplace (art, furniture, jewellery) [6]. 
- **Growth:** From a Paris-focused "marché aux puces" inspiration to a global platform with >1M registered dealers [6]. 

---

## 6. MutualArt

- **Founded:** 2008 by Moti Shniberg & Al Brenner [7]. 
- **Differentiator:** Real-time data aggregation from 40,000+ venues; AI-enhanced alerts for collectors and professionals [7]. 

---

## 7. Artfacts

- **Founded:** 2001 by Marek Claassen & Stine Albertsen [8]. 
- **Unique Value:** The artist-ranking system and AI-advisor **Limna** (launched 2021) [8]. 

---

## 8. ArtRabbit

- **Founded:** Year not publicly disclosed (company reports "Founded on the principle that art should be accessible to everyone") [9]. 
- **Service:** Free event-discovery platform; crowdsourced event listings; tools for curators and artists [9]. 

---

## 9. Widewalls

- **Founded:** 2017 in London, UK [10] [11]. 
- **Product Suite:** News portal, marketplace, auction-tracking API, and a modern-art-focused editorial brand [10]. 
- **Funding:** $890k seed round (2021) [11]. 

---

## 10. Artsper

- **Founded:** 2013 in Paris, France [12]. 
- **Model:** Curated European online shop; partnership network of >1,500 galleries [13]. 
- **Catalogue:** ~130,000 works [14]. 

---

## Authoritative Reference
