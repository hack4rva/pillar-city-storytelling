Prior Art E5 — What Is Weekend-Viable Based on Prior Art

Based on the prior art survey (E1-E4), assess which solution patterns from the benchmark scan can realistically be built by a small mixed team in 48 hours.

Evaluate each option:
- Arts event aggregator (Eventbrite API + RSS feeds)
- Neighborhood discovery feed (geographic filter on aggregated events)
- Arts and culture district map (geographic visualization)
- Story collection portal (guided prompts, voice or text input, consent flow)
- Story-to-insight dashboard (theme extraction from submitted stories)
- Neighborhood history explorer (place-based stories, seeded content)

For each option, assess:
- Technical complexity on a 1-5 scale
- Data availability on a 1-5 scale
- Demo quality achievable in 48 hours on a 1-5 scale
- Institutional dependencies on a 1-5 scale (higher = more dependencies = riskier for weekend)

Output
- Ranked list of options by weekend viability
- Recommended primary MVP shape for a team that wants the best chance of a strong demo
- Stretch goal recommendations if the primary shape is completed early
- Specific risks to watch for in the top recommended option
