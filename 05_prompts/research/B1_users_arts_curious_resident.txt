Users B1 — The Arts-Curious Richmond Resident

Build a detailed persona for a Richmond resident who wants to discover and attend local arts and cultural events but currently struggles to find them. Include:

- Demographic range (new residents, longtime residents who are not plugged in, visitors)
- Current discovery behaviors: which channels they use, which they find unreliable, which they wish existed
- Specific pain points: what events do they miss and why
- Technology comfort level: what devices and platforms they use naturally
- What "success" looks like for them in a discovery tool

Base on evidence from Richmond, Virginia and comparable cities. Label inferences clearly.

Output
- Persona profile with behaviors, pain points, and goals
- Specific journey failure points this persona experiences today
- Feature requirements implied by this persona's needs
- What this persona would find in a successful tool that does not exist today
