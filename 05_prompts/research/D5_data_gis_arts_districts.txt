Data D5 — Richmond GIS Data for Arts and Cultural Districts

Investigate what geographic data exists in the Richmond GeoHub and other public GIS sources that is relevant to arts and cultural district mapping. Specifically:

- Does Richmond GeoHub (rvagis.richmond.gov) have a layer for arts and cultural districts or designated arts zones?
- What neighborhood boundary data exists in GeoHub and is it downloadable as GeoJSON or Shapefile?
- What other relevant geographic layers exist: historic districts, parks/cultural facilities, business improvement districts?
- Are Richmond's formally designated arts districts (if any) documented in GeoHub or another official source?
- How current and maintained is the geographic data in GeoHub?

Output
- Confirmed GIS layers available from Richmond GeoHub relevant to this pillar
- Data formats available and download access
- Recommendation: should a map-based arts tool use arts district boundaries (if they exist) or neighborhood boundaries (more reliable)?
- Fallback strategy if arts district data is not confirmed in GeoHub
