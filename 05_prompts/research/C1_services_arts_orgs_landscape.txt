Services C1 — Richmond Arts Organizations Landscape

Map the landscape of arts organizations, galleries, venues, and cultural institutions in Richmond, Virginia. Focus on:

- Major institutions (VMFA, ICA at VCU, Richmond Symphony, Richmond Ballet, Firehouse Theatre, etc.)
- Mid-size organizations (1708 Gallery, Visual Arts Center, Virginia Rep, etc.)
- Community and neighborhood arts spaces
- Arts organizations serving underrepresented communities (African American, Latino, LGBTQ+ communities)
- How each category typically promotes events (social media, email lists, Eventbrite, their own websites, RSS/iCal feeds)

Output
- Categorized inventory of Richmond arts organizations by type and size
- How they currently publish event information (channels and formats)
- Which have likely structured calendar data (RSS, iCal, Eventbrite) vs. which are informal
- Which organizations serve underrepresented communities and may be harder to aggregate
- Gaps in what an aggregator would cover vs. not cover
