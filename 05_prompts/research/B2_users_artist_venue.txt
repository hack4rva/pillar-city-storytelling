Users B2 — The Local Artist or Small Venue

Build a detailed persona for a Richmond artist or small arts venue that wants greater visibility for their events without additional administrative burden. Include:

- Type of organization (solo artist, gallery, small theater, community arts space, etc.)
- Current promotion practices: which platforms they use, how much time they spend, what they get in return
- Pain points: reaching new audiences, maintaining multiple calendars, missing discoverability
- What they would accept vs. what they would resist from an aggregation tool (consent, attribution, control)
- Technology capacity: do they have staff who can maintain a website calendar? A reliable RSS feed?

Base on evidence from Richmond, Virginia and comparable small arts communities. Label inferences clearly.

Output
- Persona profile with current promotion behaviors and pain points
- What this persona gains and risks from an aggregator that pulls their event data
- What attribution and consent requirements they would likely demand
- Feature requirements for a tool to work for this user without adding burden
