Services C5 — Where Residents Fall Through the Cracks

Synthesize across the arts discovery and civic storytelling landscapes to identify where Richmond residents currently fall through the cracks. Focus on:

- The resident who wants to attend arts events but cannot find them: where exactly does the discovery chain break?
- The longtime resident who wants to share neighborhood history: where does the sharing chain break?
- Community organizations doing adjacent work that do not connect to City processes
- Arts organizations that produce events but reach only their existing networks

Identify the specific "crack" moments — the exact point in each user's journey where they give up or are failed.

Output
- Crack inventory: specific failure moments for each user type
- Service and infrastructure gaps that cause the cracks
- Which gaps are addressable by a weekend hackathon prototype
- Which gaps require institutional solutions beyond a hackathon
