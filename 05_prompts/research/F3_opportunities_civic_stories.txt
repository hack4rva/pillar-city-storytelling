Opportunity Spaces F3 — Civic Storytelling Deep Dive

For the civic storytelling opportunity specifically, map out what a successful hackathon prototype would look like in detail. Address:

- What is the minimum viable story collection tool: what features are required for a compelling demo?
- What guided prompts would resonate most with Richmond residents, particularly longtime community members?
- What does the consent flow look like in plain language for a non-technical user?
- How does voice input work in the browser, and what are the technical requirements?
- What happens after a story is submitted — how is the "use pathway" communicated to the user?
- What seeded stories would make the demo most credible if live submissions are sparse?

Output
- Minimum viable feature set for a credible story collection demo
- 5 suggested guided prompts tailored to Richmond neighborhoods and history
- Consent language template in plain English (and ideally Spanish)
- Voice recording implementation notes (browser API, fallback options)
- Demo strategy if real submissions have not been received
