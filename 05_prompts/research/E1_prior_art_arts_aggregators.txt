Prior Art E1 — City Arts Event Aggregators: National Examples

Research city-specific arts and cultural event aggregators that exist nationally. For each, describe what they aggregate, how they are maintained, who owns them, and whether they are sustainable.

Focus on:
- Do Richmond (dorichmond.com): existing Richmond tool — how does it work, what does it cover, is it maintained?
- Chicago Office of Cultural Affairs digital tools
- DC-area arts aggregators (ArtsInDC, similar)
- Boston area arts discovery tools
- Philadelphia arts and culture portals
- Any city that has built a sustainable automated arts event aggregator

Also: what happened to Eventful and similar national tools that tried comprehensive event aggregation?

Output
- Inventory of national arts aggregator examples with sustainability notes
- Key design and technical lessons from successful tools
- Key failure patterns from tools that decayed or shut down
- Assessment of what makes an arts aggregator sustainable vs. what causes it to fail
- Recommendation for which models to emulate for a Richmond weekend prototype
