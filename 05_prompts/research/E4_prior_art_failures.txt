Prior Art E4 — What Failed and Why

Research documented failures of city event calendars and civic story collection tools. Identify what went wrong, what the lessons are, and how to avoid repeating them.

Specific failure patterns to investigate:
- City events calendars that decayed due to manual curation burden (document specific examples)
- Story collection portals that collected submissions but produced no visible civic use (document specific examples)
- Arts aggregators that failed to reach underrepresented arts communities
- Tools that were technically functional but failed to build trust with skeptical resident populations
- National tool shutdowns: Eventful, Plancast, other event aggregators that ceased operation

Output
- Documented failure cases with root cause analysis
- Key failure patterns specific to arts aggregation
- Key failure patterns specific to story collection
- Specific design and institutional choices that prevented or caused failures
- Checklist of failure conditions to test your hackathon prototype against
